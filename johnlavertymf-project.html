John Laverty Motor Factors

Signage & Graphic Design Work for Website and Social Media
Client: John Laverty Motor Factors

Project Overview:

With over 25 years experience in the automotive industry, John Laverty set up his own motor factor outlet in 1999. The first premises was located in Linenhall Street in the centre of Dundalk and John Laverty Motor Factors quickly became the leading motor factor in Dundalk and the surrounding area. In 2008, they expanded the business and moved to a purpose built 4,000 sq meter premises on the Coes Road in Dundalk. John Laverty Motor Factors stocks thousands of automotive parts that serve every major car, van and truck on the road. They provide motorists with the largest range of automotive products and parts available at competitive prices.

What is the key objective?

The main purpose of the signage at John Laverty's shop is to communicate and convey information designed to assist the receiver with decision-making based on the information provided and also persuade customers of the merits of a given product or service. The main objective of working on John Laverty's social media was to increase brand awareness, drive traffic to his website, generate new leads, grow his revenue and boost brand engagement

Timeline:

The shop signage had a deadline of 3 weeks. Contacting a local signage company around Dundalk and getting them to measure out the windows was the first process. Once I had the measurments, I was able to go ahead with the design process and make a number of different mock ups so the customer could pick one that he liked. Once he agreed a design, I could sent it off to the printers and it took them a week to print and put the signage up in his shop. The social media and wesbite work is an ongoing project. This included social media marketing, design ideation, illustrations, low fidelity mockups, print design and email marketing.
