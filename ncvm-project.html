National Computer & Videogame Museum

Website Design & Development/Logo Design
Client: National Computer and Videogame Museum    Website: ncvm.ie

Project Overview:

The National Computer and Videogame Museum are actively involved in the research, documentation, preservation and promotion of the history of the computer and videogame industries in Ireland. The client was in need of a brochure website, displaying all their information and contact details so clients and fans could get in touch with them easily, as well as displaying all their latest news and events. NCVM were also in need of a new logo that accurately represented their company.

What is the key objective?

The goals of creating a Brochure Website for NCVM was to provide information about their business, products and services to customers, give potential customers an easy way to find their contact details, increase the credibility of their company, creating a website helped build trust with customers as it shows personal investment in their business, showed off their startup business to investors and helped present their professional imagery to their clients.

The client was also in need of a logo that accurately represented their business, while appealing to potential customers who would see it for the first time.

Timeline:

The project had to be completed within 1 and a half months. This included logo design, design mockups, wireframing/prototyping, social media marketing, website testing/analytics.

Team + My Role:

A Web/Graphic Designer (myself) and a Web Developer. The team worked together on research, website development, interaction design, marketing and user testing. I believe some of my strengths are in the graphic design and web development as well as visual design and prototyping aspects of design.
