Eure!Parts Magazine

Trade Magazine for the Automotive Aftermarket
Client: Eure!Parts Magazine

Project Overview:

The Eure!Parts magazine is special concept developed exclusively for the Irish automotive aftermarket, released this the market ever quarter, that brings together top suppliers of parts, equipment and workshop consumables, with leading independent motor factors, in a programme that offers a complete package of first class product, service, support and value for garages.

What is the key objective?

The main objective of the Eure!Parts trade publication is to keep members of the automotive aftermarket industry up to date with the newest information and special offers. This promotional magazine is targeted to mechanics and tradesmen across Ireland, and is distributed throughout Motor Factors nationwide. During advertising campaigns, there are 2,000 copies of the magazine distributed across the country. Timeline:

The process in creating the trade magazine takes about a months work. This includes liaising with the purchasing managers and sales teams and deciding what products and promotions will go in. Once prices and part numbers have been agreed, I will begin the design process. I follow the same format for all the Eure!Parts magazines we produce, so to keep good consistency throughout every issue. Once adverts and artwork has been signed, I will send the artwork to the printers so the magazines can be produced.
