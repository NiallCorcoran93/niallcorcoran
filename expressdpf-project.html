Express DPF Cleaning

Website Design & Development
Client: Express DPF Cleaning     Website: expressdpfcleaning.ie

Project Overview:

Express DPF Cleaning is a new service for garages that has just been launched as a joint venture by three leading Dublin motor factors. It aims to provide garages with a convenient and cost effective alternative to expensive DPF replacement, when the filter has become clogged and normal regeneration is no longer effective. The companies behind the new service are Finglas Autoparts, Tallaght Autoparts and Ballyfermot Autoparts. Eugene Hilliard of Finglas Autoparts says,”Clogged DPFs are now a huge issue for our customers, often meaning NCT failure. Replacing a DPF can be very costly so we set out to find an economical but very effective alternative that could provide big cost savings and boost customer satisfaction.”

What is the key objetive?

The key objective here was to create a brochure website for Express DPf Cleaning and provide them with an online platform so customers, old and new, could get in touch with them and view all of their services. Their website also mobile friendly and included features like photo gallerys and contact forms.
