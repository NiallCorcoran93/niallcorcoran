JLM Lubricants

Vehicle Signage
Client: JLM Lubricants

Project Overview:

Established in 2010, based in the manufacturing powerhouse of The Netherlands, JLM Lubricants is a global brand that designs, develops and manufactures a wide range of premium products for the automotive industry. JLM products are primarily developed for the professional motor mechanic working in an increasingly diagnostics based environment. JLM Lubricants have distributors in more than 40 countries and rising. The JLM team develop products that keep internal combustion engines in peak form. Their fuel additives have a dual capacity – preventative and problem solving; they maintain the lowest CO2 emissions possible and reduce or eliminate the need for repairs. The JLM range includes TUV approved Valve Saver products, DPF cleaners with cerium and platinum and the OEM standard JLM Advance engine oil.

What is the key objective?

The objective of this project was to create vehicle signage for the new JLM Lubricants pick up that would be travelling around the length and breadth of Ireland, using it as a marketing tool for the JLM brand. I had to create a number of different mock-ups and drafts, until it was decided upon that a camouflage style would be the best look for the pick up. Once the design was agreed, I got in contact with the vehicle signage company we were working with and within 2 weeks, the pick up was ready to hit the roads!
