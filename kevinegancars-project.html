Kevin Egan Cars

Graphic Design Work for Website and Social Media
Client: Kevin Egan Cars   

Project Overview:

Kevin Egan Cars is a Premier Used Car dealer & Since 2018 have been Appointed the main Peugeot Dealership for Sligo, Leitrim & Roscommon. At Kevin Egan Cars, Sligo they specialise in providing assured, top quality pre-owned vehicles. Their aim is to bring guaranteed quality used cars to the Irish Market with un-rivaled standards in car preparation, presentation, and most importantly price. Kevin Egan Cars Also Stock the complete range of Peugeot Cars & Commercials from the ever-popular Peugeot 3008 to the legend that is the Peugeot Partner van, and offer competitive trade-in prices and offer some of the best finance deals available.

What is the key objetive?

The key objective of the project was to create engaging, advertising material for both website and social media platforms and to gather data analytics on how much traffic these designs generated.
