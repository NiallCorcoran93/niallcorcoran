TaskMe App

Task Managing Mobile App for Students
Project: TaskMe

Project Overview:

TaskMe is an online task managing online web application for students, both second and third level students. TaskMe allows students to plan out their study week in advance.

What is the key objective?

This project was exclusively designed to simplify the tracking and monitoring of day-to-day activities of the busy modern life of second and third level students. Students are so busy in their daily lives right now that it becomes impossible to keep track of daily events, meetings, and assignments. To address this issue, this project focuses on developing a daily task managing System for Android devices that allows the user to plan out their weekly events in an organised and effectived way.

Timeline:

This was a year long project during my final year of college, studying software development level 8 and had to be completed within a 6 month period. This included user research, presentating, project write up, design ideation, wireframing, low fidelity mockups, email marketing, social media marketing, testing and finally high fidelity testing.

Team + My Role:

A Web/Graphic Designer (myself) and a Software Developer. The team worked together on research, website development, interaction design, marketing and user testing. I believe some of my strengths are in the graphic design and web development as well as visual design and prototyping aspects of design.
