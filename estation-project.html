e-station

Website Design/Development & Logo Design
Client: e-station    Website: e-station.ie

Project Overview:

e-station are a business that supply, install and support Electric Vehicle Charging Solutions for Residential, Commercial and Public Sector clients. They provide a range of equipment designed to meet the varied needs of their customers, from simple “Plug & Play” solutions to more sophisticated networked systems. These systems can be metered and managed by a suite of software accessed by PC, tablet or smartphone and allow for the consumption of electricity to be managed and monetised. e-station wanted to move their business online, so they required a website that allowed customers to purchased all of their electric charging solutions form the click of a button and be delivered to their residency or business.

What is the key objetive?

Ecommerce business drives profitable growth with reduction is cost-to-customer, developing customer-reach, and providing a unique customer experience. So of the key objectives for providing an ecommerce platform for e-station included: reduce management costs, develop new business relations, provide a unique customer experience, increase the number of loyal customers, boost the efficiency of services and increase their overall sales.

Timeline:

We had a total of 3 months to complete this project. This included user research, design ideation, wireframing, low fidelity mockups, email marketing, social media marketing, testing and finally high fidelity testing.

Team + My Role:

A Web/Graphic Designer (myself) and a Marketing Specialist. The team worked together on research, website development, interaction design, marketing and user testing. I believe some of my strengths are in the graphic design and web development as well as visual design and prototyping aspects of design.
