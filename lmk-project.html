LMK Hair

Social Media Marketing & Print Design
Client: LMK Hair

Project Overview:

LMK Hair is a hair/beauty salon that is located in Ballinagh Town, 10 minutes outside Cavan Town. The owner Leeanne, has been running her business since 2018 and she has recently wanted to increase her presence online and on social media. Professional business cards and gift vouchers were also created.

What is the key objective?

The main objective of this endeavour was to help improve LMK Hair's social media footpath, gathering more Facebook and Instagram followers, while reaching a greater audience/potential customers.

Timeline:

This is an ongoing project. This included social media marketing, design ideation, illustrations, low fidelity mockups, print design and email marketing.
