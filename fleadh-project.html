Fleadh Cheoil na hÉireann 2015

Weekly Events Calendar
Client: Fleadh Cheoil na hÉireann

Project Overview:

Fleadh Cheoil na hÉireann is the biggest traditional Irish music festival on the planet, a week-long jamboree of music, song and dance, not to mention late nights and family fun! While attending college in Institute of Technology Sligo, I partook in a group project, where we designed and developed an online application to allow members to create daily events.

What is the key objective?

The main objective of project was the create an online calendar application, that allowed commitee members of the Fleadh to view/create/edit or delete weekly events, including daily music competitions and family events.

Timeline:

This project lasted 6 months. This included UI/UX Design, prototyping, graphic design, testing and presenting to the client.

Team + My Role:

A Web/Graphic Designer (myself), a Web Devleoper and a Software Developer. The team worked together on research, website development, interaction design, marketing and user testing. I believe some of my strengths are in the graphic design and web development as well as visual design and prototyping aspects of design.
