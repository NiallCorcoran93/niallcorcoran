J&S Automotive Distirbutors

Graphic Design/Video Editing/Marketing Work
Client: J&S Automotive Distributors

Project Overview:

J&S Automotive Distributors are based in Dublin in the Republic of Ireland. They specialise in car part distribution and have continued to grow year on year, since our formation in 1977. They sell and deliver the highest quality car parts. All the products that they distribute are catalogued on TecDoc and MAM Autonet. We pride ourselves on our flexibility. J&S process well over 100 orders per hour and this is fully integrated with their delivery system. J&S are proud to be a well-managed family owned business with a robust balance sheet, enviable track record and a policy of on-going improvement of all their procedures.

What is the key objective?

The objective was to help promote the J&S through different forms of media, including Social Media, print, promotional videos and signage.
