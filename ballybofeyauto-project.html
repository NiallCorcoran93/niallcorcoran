Ballybofey Autofactors

Graphic Design & Digital Marketing Work for Website, Social Media & Shop Signage
Client: Ballybofey Autofactors   

Project Overview:

Established in 2001, Ballybofey Auto Factors has become one of Donegals leading retailers in car parts and accessories, supplying the North West. They have a wide range of parts & accessories for cars and trailers from brands such as Sealey, Valvoline, AutoGlym etc. in stock. We pride ourselves on our Service both Sales and After Sales. Ballybofey Autofactors are on of the few factors across Ireland, that sell online as well as instore. They have a large customer base in the North West of Ireland and wanted to reach a wider auidence, by increasing their online presence, through their wesbite, social media pages and google advertisements. Ballybofey Autofactors also run their yearly Black Friday event during the month of November.

What is the key objetive?

The key objectives of working with Ballybofey Autofactors were to generate more leads online, improve their conversion rate (convert more readers/ viewers/followers into customers), increase the companies sales, increase traffic through website, organic traffic and traffic from social media.

Timeline:

Ballybofey's Black Friday event takes ever November and at least two month's planning needs to go into promoting and advertising the event. Other work such as Facebook Cover Photos and Building Signage needs at least 2-3 weeks work to create mock-ups/proofs before being put into action.
